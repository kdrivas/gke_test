Types of Coffee

  • Espresso

    A full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee served in “shots.”

  • Americano

    A style of coffee prepared by adding hot water to espresso.

  • Latte

    A coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.

  • Cappuccino

    An espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy, and is traditionally prepared with steamed milk foam.

  • Mocha

    A chocolate-flavored variant of a caffè latte.

  • Macchiato

    An espresso coffee drink with a small amount of milk, usually foamed.
